🔐 **RBAC vs ABAC - Access Control Models Explained!** 🛡️⚖️

Understanding the difference between Role-Based and Attribute-Based Access Control is critical for building secure applications. Which one fits your use case?

✅ RBAC — permissions based on predefined roles and job functions
✅ ABAC — dynamic access based on user, resource, and environment attributes
✅ RBAC is simpler and easier to manage for small teams
✅ ABAC offers fine-grained control for complex environments
✅ Choose based on your scalability and security requirements
