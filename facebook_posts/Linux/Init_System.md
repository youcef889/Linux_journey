⚙️ **Linux Init Systems Explained – SysVinit, systemd, runit!** 🐧🔧

Ever wondered what happens from the moment you press the power button to when you see your login screen? The init system is PID 1 and the backbone of every Linux distribution. Let's break down the most popular ones!

✅ SysVinit – The classic sequential init
✅ systemd – Modern parallel service manager
✅ runit – Minimal and fast daemon supervision
✅ OpenRC – Flexible dependency-based init
✅ Upstart – Event-driven init by Ubuntu
