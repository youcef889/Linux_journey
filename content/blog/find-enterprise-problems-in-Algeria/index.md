If you want to **find enterprise problems in Algeria**, you need to understand the **main difficulties companies face in the Algerian market**. These are real problems you can build solutions for (software, automation, services, etc.).

Here are some **major problems enterprises face in Algeria** 👇

---

# 1️⃣ Bureaucracy and Administrative Processes

One of the biggest problems is **slow bureaucracy**.

Companies struggle with:

* business registration
* permits and licenses
* customs procedures
* government approvals

These processes can take a long time and slow business operations. ([ASJP][1])

💡 **Opportunity**
Build solutions like:

* digital document management
* business workflow automation
* government form automation tools

---

# 2️⃣ Limited Access to Funding

Many companies and startups **cannot get loans or investment**.

Reasons:

* strict bank requirements
* few venture capital investors
* limited startup ecosystem ([ASJP][1])

💡 **Opportunity**
Products to build:

* crowdfunding platforms
* SME financing platforms
* investment marketplaces

---

# 3️⃣ Poor Digital Infrastructure

Many companies still operate with **manual processes and outdated systems**.

Problems include:

* unreliable internet in some areas
* low adoption of digital tools
* weak telecom infrastructure ([ASJP][2])

💡 **Opportunity**
Build:

* business management software
* cloud tools for SMEs
* ERP systems for small companies

---

# 4️⃣ Logistics and Distribution Problems

Companies often struggle to **deliver products across the country**.

Problems:

* weak transportation infrastructure
* high delivery costs
* difficulty reaching rural areas ([ASJP][2])

💡 **Opportunity**
Build:

* delivery management platforms
* logistics optimization software
* warehouse management systems

---

# 5️⃣ Payment Systems

Many businesses rely heavily on **cash payments**.

This creates problems for:

* e-commerce
* online services
* subscriptions

Community discussions also highlight that **payment systems and trust online are still difficult** in Algeria. ([Reddit][3])

💡 **Opportunity**
Build:

* digital payment platforms
* escrow systems for marketplaces
* invoicing software

---

# 6️⃣ Import and Trade Restrictions

Companies importing goods face:

* customs delays
* complex import rules
* currency exchange restrictions ([NABC][4])

💡 **Opportunity**
Build:

* import management platforms
* customs document automation
* trade compliance software

---

# 7️⃣ Talent and Skills Gap

Companies struggle to find:

* skilled engineers
* experienced developers
* technical specialists ([prezi.com][5])

💡 **Opportunity**
Build:

* technical training platforms
* job matching systems
* developer outsourcing platforms

---

# 8️⃣ Weak Internal Systems in Companies

Many Algerian companies still use:

* Excel
* paper documents
* manual workflows

This creates inefficiencies.

💡 **Opportunity**
Build software like:

* CRM systems
* HR management systems
* accounting automation
* inventory software
