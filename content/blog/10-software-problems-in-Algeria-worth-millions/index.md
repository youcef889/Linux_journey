Here are **10 real software problems in Algeria that could be worth millions** if someone builds the right solution. These come from current issues in Algerian businesses, digital infrastructure, and the growing tech market.

---

# 1️⃣ Digital Payments Infrastructure

Most Algerians still pay **cash on delivery**, especially in e-commerce. ([Algeria Invest][1])

Problems:

* Low trust in online payments
* Few international payment gateways
* High friction for online businesses

💡 Software opportunity

* Local **Stripe-like payment gateway**
* **Mobile wallet** for businesses
* **Payment APIs for developers**

Potential market: **e-commerce ($1.5B+ market)**.

---

# 2️⃣ Logistics & Delivery Management

Delivery companies often operate with **manual systems**.

Problems:

* No real-time package tracking
* Manual order management
* Poor route planning ([Symloop][2])

💡 Software opportunity

* Delivery fleet management SaaS
* Route optimization systems
* Driver mobile apps

This is huge because **e-commerce depends on logistics**.

---

# 3️⃣ Document Management Systems

Many companies still use **paper documents and WhatsApp** to manage files.

Problems:

* Lost documents
* No version control
* Slow approval processes ([DocuClair][3])

💡 Software opportunity

* Cloud **document management system (DMS)**
* Digital approval workflows
* Contract tracking

This can improve productivity **40-60% in some companies**. ([DocuClair][3])

---

# 4️⃣ SME ERP Systems

Most Algerian SMEs run their businesses using:

* Excel
* paper
* manual processes

Problems:

* no integrated systems
* poor data visibility

💡 Software opportunity
Build a **simple ERP for SMEs**:

Modules:

* accounting
* inventory
* HR
* invoices

---

# 5️⃣ Trust Infrastructure for E-commerce

Trust is one of the biggest barriers to online transactions.

Problems:

* fraud fear
* fake sellers
* disputes between buyers and sellers ([Unb][4])

💡 Software opportunity

* **Escrow payment systems**
* verified seller platforms
* dispute resolution platforms

---

# 6️⃣ Accounting & Tax Automation

Accounting in Algeria is still very manual.

Problems:

* accountants exchange files via email
* scattered financial data
* penalties due to mistakes

💡 Software opportunity

* Algerian-compliant accounting SaaS
* automated tax filing
* invoice scanning (AI OCR)

---

# 7️⃣ Supply Chain & Import Management

Algerian businesses importing goods face:

* customs paperwork
* regulatory complexity
* slow processes

💡 Software opportunity

* import documentation platforms
* customs compliance tools
* supplier tracking systems

---

# 8️⃣ Recruitment & Talent Platforms

Companies struggle to find skilled tech workers.

Reasons:

* shortage of trained engineers
* mismatch between education and industry needs ([Digtial Arabia Network][5])

💡 Software opportunity

* developer talent marketplace
* skill testing platforms
* hiring automation tools

---

# 9️⃣ Data Analytics for SMEs

Many businesses **do not analyze their data**.

Problems:

* no dashboards
* no performance analytics
* decisions based on intuition

💡 Software opportunity

* plug-and-play business dashboards
* AI analytics for SMEs
* sales prediction tools

---

# 🔟 Informal Economy Digitization

A large part of Algeria’s economy is **informal**.

Problems:

* no invoices
* no digital records
* no credit history

💡 Software opportunity

* micro-business apps
* digital invoicing systems
* business identity platforms
