# 1️⃣ SME ERP for Algerian Businesses

Most small and medium businesses in Algeria run their operations with **Excel, notebooks, or paper**.

## The Problem

Companies struggle to manage:

* inventory
* accounting
* employee records
* invoices
* purchases

Everything is fragmented.

### The Market Context

Algeria has **1.36 million SMEs** (as of 2022), representing a 5.71% year-over-year growth. However, fewer than 20% of Algerian SMEs are digitally engaged, meaning the vast majority still operate with traditional methods.

Key challenges include:

* Access to finance (only 43% financial inclusion rate)
* Limited managerial skills and qualified staff
* Low adoption of new technologies
* Bureaucratic barriers and heavy taxation procedures
* Difficulty entering both local and international markets

The Algerian government has recognized these challenges and is actively pushing digital transformation. The Ministry of SME Development has launched initiatives to support digitization, including grants for management software, e-commerce platforms, and digital solutions.

## SaaS Solution

A **simple cloud ERP designed specifically for Algerian SMEs**.

### Core Features

**Accounting & Finance:**
* General ledger with DZD currency support
*accounts payable/receivable tracking
* Algerian tax compliance (TVA 19%, with sector-specific rates)
* Multi-currency support for imports/exports

**Inventory Management:**
* Stock tracking across multiple warehouses
* Low-stock alerts and reorder points
* Barcode/SKU management
* Batch and expiry date tracking

**Invoicing & Billing:**
* Compliant Algerian invoice generation
* Automatic tax calculations
* Recurring invoice templates
* PDF export with legal formatting

**HR & Payroll:**
* Employee database management
* Attendance tracking
* Salary calculation with legal deductions
* Contract management

**Supplier Management:**
* Supplier database with contact info
* Purchase order creation and tracking
* Delivery scheduling
* Payment terms tracking

**Analytics Dashboard:**
* Real-time business overview
* Profit/loss statements
* Cash flow projections
* Growth trends

### Localization Features

* **Bilingual UI**: Arabic (right-to-left) + French interfaces
* **Algerian Tax Rules**: Built-in compliance withDirection Générale des Impôts (DGI) regulations
* **DZD Currency**: Native support with formatting
* **Local Payment Integration**: Baridimob, CCP, and banking connectors
* **Regulatory Updates**: Automated compliance with changing tax laws
